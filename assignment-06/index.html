French Toast Souffle


NOTE: This recipe involves letting a dish sit overnight!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 2 loaves cubed French Bread
  • 12 eggs
  • 3 cups half-and-half
  • 1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
  • cinnamon
  • powdered sugar

Directions

  1. Coat 2 baking dishes with butter and fill half full with bread cubes.
  2. In a bowl, mix softened butter, cream cheese, and syrup.
  3. Spoon mixture over cubes and spread evenly.
  4. In a bowl, beat eggs, half-and-half, and vanilla.
  5. Pour egg mixture over bread - make sure all bread cubes are moistened.
  6. Dust with cinnamon, cover, and store overnight in refrigerator.
  7. In the morning, bake for 55-60 minutes in a 350Â°F oven.
  8. Dust with powdered sugar.
