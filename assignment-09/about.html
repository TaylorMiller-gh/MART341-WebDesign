Why create Pixel Art "Infinite" Tiles

My history with pixel art.

To be perfectly honest I'm quite terrible at pixel art! I picked it up a few years ago when the programmer of our game studio said he would be really happy if he could take part in a pixel-style game. I had no experience, but I wanted to contribute to a project we were all happy about. Making tiling textures is a really cool skill I learned that anyone can do. Things start to get tricky when you need to have edges like the image used for the nav bar. Regardless, it's a fast way to make tiles - I don't know if you're in to making pixel art, but now you have one more wizard spell added to your inventory.

Where can you find more?

Before going to school, I learned everything I know about creating digital art through Youtube and Udemy, an online course website. The most difficult part is setting a goal and teasing apart how you want to solve each problem to any project. Once you have a technique in mind, check the web. Maybe someone has a solution or an idea to get the ball rolling.
